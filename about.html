We love making
your life easier and
‍our cities greener

We are on a mission to save you time and make your life easier. We have reinvented the way goods and services are delivered with an innovative and reliable self-service pickup network, making our cities smarter and greener.

Alfred was born to make deliveries efficient and sustainable.

We are going green, building a zero-emissions delivery network across 18 districts and 500 Alfred lockers and pickup points in Hong Kong.

We are committed to:

Cleaner fuel

Using a delivery fleet of only electric vans by 2023.

Sustainability

Providing only biodegradable delivery bags to our online merchants.

Recycling

Producing courier uniforms using recycled ocean plastics.
